Michael Baum - Wrestler
Michael is a professional wrestler with 20 years experience training wrestlers all around the United States and abroad.

His life's work has been dedicated to the field of olympic, professional, and entertainment wrestling.

He's produced a generation of world champions, including Marchov Kabron, James Well, and Ni Lao.



Midas Touch
Standing at 6'2" and over 300 lbs, Michael is a heavyweight wrestler that has the speed and precision of a lightweight.

He's defeated hundreds of opponents on the national and international stage, including the American All Rounder 1999 and the Shanghai Finals 2003.
